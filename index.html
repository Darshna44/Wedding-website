Our Story

Two Cultures, One Love

Our love story is beautifully woven with traditions from both our cultures. Darshna brings the rich heritage of Indian customs, while Michael embraces these traditions with an open heart.

This Indian wedding celebration honors Darshna's family traditions and allows us to share the beauty of Indian customs with all our loved ones.

Why Two Celebrations?

We chose to have two wedding celebrations to honor both our backgrounds and give our families the opportunity to celebrate in ways that are meaningful to them.

Our Indian wedding will feature traditional ceremonies, vibrant colors, and customs that have been passed down through generations in Darshna's family.

Q & A

What should I wear to an Indian wedding?
Traditional Indian attire is preferred and encouraged! Think saris, lehengas, or salwar kameez for women, and kurtas or sherwanis for men. Bright colors are welcome and celebrated.
How long will the ceremony be?
Indian wedding ceremonies typically last 2-3 hours with various rituals and traditions. There will be explanations provided for those unfamiliar with the customs.
Will there be vegetarian food?
Yes! The entire menu will be vegetarian, following traditional Indian wedding customs. There will be a wide variety of delicious dishes to enjoy.
Do I need to remove my shoes?
You may need to remove shoes when entering certain areas of the ceremony space. We'll provide clear guidance on the day.
Can I take photos during the ceremony?
We'll have a professional photographer, but you're welcome to take photos during most parts of the ceremony. We'll let you know if there are any specific moments when we'd prefer no photos.
What if I don't understand the rituals?
Don't worry! We'll have someone explaining the significance of different rituals throughout the ceremony so everyone can appreciate the beautiful traditions.
